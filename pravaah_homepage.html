THEME:PRISM

“Prism blends these sub-themes—Space (infinity), Retro (heritage), and Quant (intellect)—into a spectrum of experiences, symbolizing the diversity and brilliance of your fest. It reflects how every shade of creativity finds its place in unity. Just like a prism turns light into colors, our fest turns ideas into unforgettable moments.”
